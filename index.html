About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Dr. K. H. Wandra , Birth date 10th September, 1974, Surendranagar (Gujarat), India. Ph.D. from Saurashtra University, Rajkot, On "Role of Wireless Technology for Mobile Augmented Reality System(MARS)", done M.E. Microprocessor System Applications from M.S. University, Vadodara. in year 1999 & B.E. in Electronics & Communication form North Gujarat University, Patan, in year1995.

Currently Working as Dean of Faculty of Technology and Engineering & Principal, C. U. Shah College of Engineering & Technology, Managed By: Vardhman Bharti Trust,Wadhwan City, Gujarat, (INDIA), having more than 16 years of UG & 7 years of PG(MCA and M.E.) Teaching Experience, Written more than 10 Books related to Computer & IT related area e.g (1) Java Programming Advanced Topics by CENGAGE Learning Publication, (2) Mobile Computing (3) Introduction Of Computers (4) Computer Graphics by Akshat Publication , Gujarat. Published / presented Five International and 7 national Research Papers. Guided more than 10 PG Students and more than 155 UG Students Dissertation work

Dr. K. H. Wandra is a Chairman of Board of Studies Computer Engineering At Saurashtra University, Rajkot, Core Committee member For Syllabus of UG & PG at Gujarat Technology University, Ahmedabad. Section Managing Committee Member of ISTE Gujarat Section, Life member of ISTE, member of IEEE and CSI, Interested for working in the area of Wireless communication ,Networks and Advanced Microprocessors.
